Trinovate Synergy 1.0

Details

Trinovate Synergy 1.0 is a premier hackathon and innovation fest organized by Trinity Academy of Engineering (TAE), Pune. This event is designed to bring together brilliant minds from diverse disciplines, including Engineering, Medical, Arts, Commerce, Agriculture, and MCA, to collaborate and create cutting-edge solutions for real-world challenges.

Trinovate Synergy 1.0 is not just a competition; it is an opportunity for students to innovate, connect, and grow. Participants will gain exposure to industry experts, mentors, and startup networks, fostering a culture of technological advancement and entrepreneurship.

Whether you’re a developer, designer, researcher, or entrepreneur, this event offers a unique chance to showcase your skills, collaborate with like-minded innovators, and push the boundaries of innovation. Join us to be a part of this transformational journey and shape the future with your ideas!

Main Event Details

Phase 1: Poster Presentation

Description: Showcase your innovative ideas through visually engaging posters. Present your research, projects, or solutions in a graphical format that sparks discussion and attracts attention. This is your chance to demonstrate complex concepts through creative visual storytelling.

Date: 19 March 2025, 10:00am IST

Phase 1 Result: 22 March 2025, 05:00pm IST

Phase 2: Model Presentation

Description: Present your working prototype or functional model that brings your innovative idea to life. This phase gives you the opportunity to demonstrate the practical implementation of your solution, its functionality, and real-world applicability.

Date: 11 April 2025, 10:00am IST

Location: Trinity Academy of Engineering, Pune [ see location ]

Registration Fees: 199/- per team

Team Size: 2 - 4 members
